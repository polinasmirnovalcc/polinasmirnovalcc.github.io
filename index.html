LCC International University is an American Liberal Arts school which is located in the heart of Europe. Last year I started my education at this wonderful place. My freshman year was amazing! First of all, I moved to a foreign country. It is an interesting experience for me since I became independent and responsible for my life. Secondly, I started studying at LCC. Education here is very interesting, moreover, it provides students with many opportunities for the future. Thirdly, I have met so many incredible people from all over the world. LCC has a great strong community so I can say that I am blessed to be surrounded by these people!

At the beginning of the first semester of my sophomore year, we had a trip to Kaunas. Since my cousin entered LCC this year, we were there together. Kaunas is a beautiful old Lithuanian city with a very long history. My friends and I visited many historical places: museums, castles, cathedrals. After all, we were walking in the center of Kaunas, in the old town. There were many incredible locations to take photographs. Travelling is an important part of my life. I enjoy visiting new places, meeting new people and share culture and experience with them.
